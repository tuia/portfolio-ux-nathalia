The Problem

Mantiqueira region is famous for its therapeutic waters, but tourists always mistake the region with another region near it that has a similar name. Their populations have always had some rivalry against each other. Mantiqueira waters website was part of a bigger tourism project that aimed to give a unique identity and integrate the region to welcome tourists better.

The Solution

First of all, we focused on research, getting to know numbers related to the tourism activity in the Mantiqueira area, the tourists profile and even travelling there to get to know the area better. Then, we developed personas, which included 3 different types of tourists and also some residents. With this research results, it was possible to determine some strategic definitions, such as stimulating the users to generate and upload content, showing the attractions on a map, showing events on a calendar and creating a news page.

The homepage shows different attractions accordingly to 5 different profiles: adventurous, history explorer, nature appreciator, medical waters and culinaire lover. The attractions map also have this filters, along with others such as accommodation, transport, food and general services. On the establishment's page, it was possible to leave comments, testimonials, pictures, videos and to share the content. The footer always shows informations about the region, such as its location, the cities that belong to the cluster it’s and contact channels.
