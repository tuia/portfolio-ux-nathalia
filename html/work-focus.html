The Problem

Minas Gerais State Court of Audits processes used to be made on Office softwares like Word and to have almost no standards between them. They also had no control over the conclusion time, getting stuck for months before getting finished.

The Solution

Focus's main panel provides key process information at the top, an image of the process’s location on the background and a panel to the right with strategic information to subsidize the analysis and deliberations of the court. This information is received through a partnership with Data Viva, a BigData application I am also working with.

Each department of the institution is represented with a color in the UI and has history and reports to measure how long the process is taking. When the documents are done, you can easily generate and print a PDF with a control QR code. After that, the documents are standardized and the court employees have a unique interface to work with.

These solutions intend to bring objectivity, timeliness, precision, continuity, integration, quality and standardization to the work performed at the Court of Audits.
