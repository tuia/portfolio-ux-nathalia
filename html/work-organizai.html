Description

Organizaí was a leading coupon organizer app, which my team at Dito company was responsible for. I learned a lot about startups, applications, metrics, front-end development, AB tests, strategies, SCRUM, agile and more throughout the process. We had a multidisciplinary team that encouraged everyone to try on new knowledges and skills, or anything that was needed to improve the final product. I’ve worked in product design, prototyping, UI design, front-end development, email marketing design and also a with a bit of motion graphics. By changing OrganizaI’s heading text I once doubled the conversion rate. There was also an occasion when I designed a newsletter with the best conversion rate on the company's history.
