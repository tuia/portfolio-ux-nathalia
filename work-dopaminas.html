Description

Dopaminas is a Brazilian rock band with influences from stoner, punk and classic rock. All of its musicians are female and the name is a reference to dopamine neurotransmetteur.

For the first album, entitled "When the leaves shake", I collected references from Art Nouveau and Stoner rock album covers. The songs from the album are heavy, delicate, obscure and sensitive, and so I wanted its images to be.

You can listen to their music at https://soundcloud.com/dopaminasrock.
